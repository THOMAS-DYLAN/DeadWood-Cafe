Our Story

A corner roastery that grew into something more.

Deadwood started with one grinder, a barn-salvaged counter, and the stubborn belief that great coffee shouldn't require a flight to a major city. We roast in-house, bake from scratch, and serve every cup like it matters — because it does.

We're not a chain, not a franchise, and not interested in being either. Just coffee done right, in a place worth staying.

Why Deadwood

Six things we never compromise on.

01
🌿
Ethically Sourced

We work directly with farmers across Ethiopia, Colombia, and Guatemala — paying above fair-trade rates and visiting our partners every year.

02
🔥
House Roasted

Every bean roasted on our Giesen roaster, three times a week. Nothing sits stale. Fresh coffee is the only coffee.

03
🥐
Baked Fresh Daily

Our kitchen starts at 5am. No frozen pastries, no bread from a truck. Every food item made in-house, that morning, by hand.

04
📖
Made for Staying

Real furniture. Calm music. Strong Wi-Fi. We designed the space so you never feel rushed — because great ideas need time to brew.

05
🤝
Community First

Monthly cuppings, local art shows, open-mic evenings. Deadwood is a café first, but a community anchor always.

06
♻️
Zero-Waste Kitchen

Grounds composted locally. Packaging compostable. Cartons recycled. We take our footprint seriously — one cup at a time.
